Privacy Policy for Tidget

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Overview

Tidget is an Android home screen widget app that allows users to display custom text on their home screen. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the app.

Information We Collect

Tidget itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All widget configuration data (text content, font, size, colour settings) is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

Advertising

Tidget displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. As part of ad serving, Google AdMob may collect and process the following:

Advertising ID (a resettable device identifier)
Device information (device model, OS version)
IP address
General location (country/region level, derived from IP)
Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)
This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy and Google's Advertising policies. We do not have access to this data.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time by going to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation on your Android device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data. Ad-related data described above is handled entirely by Google AdMob and subject to Google's own privacy terms.

Data Retention

Widget settings are stored locally on your device and are deleted when you uninstall the app. We retain no data on any server.

Children's Privacy

Tidget is intended for users aged 18 and over and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Contact
